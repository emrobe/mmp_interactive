*Draft quality definitions are as follows:

Finished:
Entries in MarRef are categorized as finished

High Quality Draft:
Presence of the 23S, 16S and 5S rRNA genes and at least 18 unique tRNAs. Completeness > 90 %
Contamination < 5 %

Near Complete
Completeness > 90 %
Contamination < 5 %

Medium Quality Draft:
Completeness > 50 %
Contamination < 10 %

Low Quality Draft:
Completeness < 50 %
Contamination < 10 %

Very Low Quality Draft:
Contamination > 10 %
